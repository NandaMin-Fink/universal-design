Overview

This site will go over the basics of Universal Design including what Universal Design is, why its necessary, and how it can be implemented.

As you navigate the site, take note of what aspects of Universal Design have been integrated. While may of these features may be subtle their impact is especially noticeable when using assistive technologies such as a screen reader.
